Smoothie

Description

This is the easiest recipe.

Ingredients
  • 9 ounces frozen mixed fruit(strawberries, peaches, pineapple, and mango)
  • 8 ounces coconut water
  • 1 blackberry (optional)
  • 1 strawberry (optional)
Steps
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Pour coconut water into a Vitamix blender. Add frozen fruit.
  3. Blend for 45 seconds.
  4. Pour smoothie into glass; garnish with strawberry and blackberry if desired.
